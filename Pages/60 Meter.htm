Activity on 60 Meter Band

All hams are invited to join a small group of Hams that are operating on 60 meter, upper-sideband (USB) phone.  QSO starts daily about 9:30 AM.  Look for them on the following channels; 5373 kHz (5371.5 kHz) and 5405 kHz (5403.5 kHz).  You might want to check all five channels for activities.

The federal government is the primary user and Amateur Radio is allowed as secondary user.  Power should not exceed "the equivalent of 50 W PEP transmitter output power into an antenna with a gain of 0 dBd."  Please check the ARRL website for more information and regulations regarding 60 meter band.
